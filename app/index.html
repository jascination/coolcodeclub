Code Club Cool is a once-weekly chance to learn to build websites.

It's aimed at Melbourne's young creatives, with the goal of making coding accessible and approachable.

Classes are free-form in style; sometimes we'll do self-paced tutorials, sometimes we'll do structured classes, sometimes we'll drink beers and talk about which pokemon was best (Psyduck).

Thanks! I think you're cool too.

Building websites is (a) really fun and satisfying, (b) personally empowering and (c) a lucrative career/side-job, and I'd like to share that skill with people.

If you have a friend who you want to bring along, please do - the more the merrier.

It's free! Totally free. No catches.

Classroom space is being donated by the very good people at Collective Campus, and instructors will all be volunteers.

In the future, we'll aim at launching a skill-sharing programme where you'll teach these skills to recent migrants, refugees, asylum seekers and under-privileged children.

But let's focus on teaching you the basics first.
