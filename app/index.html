Web development is a skillset built entirely on sharing.

Nearly everyone who builds websites learned using tools made by incredibly generous people who wanted to share their craft with the world.

Cool Code Club is a weekly meetup for learning web development, creating cool things and good old fashioned nerding out.

It's completely free. But there's a twist.

!!!

Shh, it's a good twist. Each week we'll ask for a donation, put towards buying laptops for underprivileged children, asylum seekers and refugees.

We'll also ask you to consider passing on your new-found skills to these people. It's easier than you think.

Who!

You! Plus James' cool mates and mates of James' cool mates!
